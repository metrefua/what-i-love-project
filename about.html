Who I Am

Hello! My name is Metrefua. This website is a reflection of what I love and enjoy.

What I Love

This website was created out of a simple love: sunsets. As a student learning web development, I wanted my first project to reflect something deeply personal and inspiring. Sunsets have always been my reminder to pause, to breathe, and to appreciate beauty even in endings. Here, I combine my growing technical skills with my passion for poetic expression. Every page is both a practice in design and a celebration of twilight’s colors. My hope is that this site not only shares my journey as a creator, but also invites you to feel the calm and wonder that sunsets bring.
